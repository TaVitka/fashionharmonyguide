Detailed Information

Personal Background

Born and raised in the vibrant city of New York, my fascination with fashion began at a young age. Growing up surrounded by diverse cultures and styles sparked my interest in the transformative power of clothing.

Professional Journey

My journey as a stylist started with a deep dive into fashion education, where I honed my skills and knowledge. Armed with a degree in Fashion Design and several certifications in personal styling, I embarked on my professional path. Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with clients from all walks of life, helping them discover their unique style identities.

Work Ethic

As a stylist, I approach each client interaction with dedication and enthusiasm. My work ethic is built on a foundation of meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to staying abreast of the latest trends. With every styling session, my goal is to instill confidence and empower individuals to embrace their authentic selves through fashion.

Personal Styling Consultation

During our Personal Styling Consultation, we provide a comprehensive analysis to understand your unique style preferences and fashion goals. This includes a detailed body type analysis, color analysis to identify your most flattering hues, and a thorough assessment of your existing wardrobe.

The consultation typically lasts between 1-2 hours, allowing ample time for us to delve into your style aspirations.

By the end of the session, you'll gain valuable insights into your personal style, empowering you to make confident fashion choices that align with your individuality.

Wardrobe Makeover

Our Wardrobe Makeover service is designed to transform your closet into a curated collection of versatile and stylish outfits. We begin with a thorough closet organization session, decluttering and reorganizing your wardrobe to maximize space and functionality.

Next, we create new outfit combinations using your existing clothing pieces and provide a personalized shopping list to fill any wardrobe gaps. The duration of the makeover varies depending on the size of your wardrobe and specific requirements.

By the end of the makeover, you'll enjoy a streamlined wardrobe filled with outfits that reflect your unique style and personality.

Personal Shopping Assistance

With our Personal Shopping Assistance service, you'll experience a personalized shopping journey tailored to your style preferences and budget. We accompany you on a shopping trip to select clothing and accessories that complement your individual style. Throughout the shopping experience, we offer expert styling tips and advice to help you make informed decisions.

The duration of the shopping assistance session depends on your shopping list and preferences.

By the end of the session, you'll walk away with a curated wardrobe that reflects your personal style and meets your fashion needs.
